A few words about myself:

A graduate of the University of California, Riverside, and Claremont Graduate University with a Doctorate of Musical Arts, I've worked for over 20 years as a guitarist, as an arranger, and as a composer, culminating in 5 years with the US Army Band Europe, performing across Europe and arranging 100's of pieces of music for live performance across the continent.

After coming back to the US, I've worked as a production music library composer, while developing my skills in code and development. My background is primarily in C# (and unity), but I love working with HTML, CSS and Javascript both with and without frameworks and libraries.
